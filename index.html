What is NASCAR?

NASCAR stands for the National Association of Stock Car Auto Racing. It is one of the most popular auto racing series, just behind the Formula One racing series. It is best known for its three national series, which are: Gander Outdoors Series (truck-like cars), Xfinity Series and Monster Energy series, the most popular being the Monster Energy series. NASCAR sanctions over 1,500 races at over 100 tracks in 48 US states as well as in Canada, Mexico, and Europe.
